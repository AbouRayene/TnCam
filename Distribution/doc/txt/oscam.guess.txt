NAME
       oscam.guess - CAID guessing table for oscam

SYNOPSIS
       CAID guessing table

DESCRIPTIONS
       <length of ECM>:<CAID>
	  CAID guessing table by len in hex, only needed for protocols (at the
	  moment BOMBA protocol only) that does not pass CAIDs

EXAMPLE
	12:3456
