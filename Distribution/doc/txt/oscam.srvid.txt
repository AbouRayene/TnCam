NAME
       oscam.srvid - service ID configuration file for oscam

SYNOPSIS
       service ID mappings

DESCRIPTIONS
       <srvid>:<name of service>
	  mapping between service ID and name of service

EXAMPLE
	000A:my service 1
	00FF:my service 2
